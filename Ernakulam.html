Ernakulam


Queen of the
          Arabian Sea.

Ernakulam is a vibrant hub brimming with culture and natural beauty. Its crown jewel, Kochi, is a captivating blend of heritage and modernity. The iconic Chinese Fishing Nets that silhouette against breathtaking sunsets on Fort Kochi Beach are a must-see. The city is adorned with historical landmarks like Mattancherry Palace, a testament to Kerala's royal past, and the Jewish Synagogue, showcasing a rich tapestry of religious diversity.

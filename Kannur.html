Kannur


Land of Looms and Lores

Kannur, a coastal gem in Kerala, India, entices visitors with its unique blend of cultural heritage and natural beauty. Renowned for its pristine beaches like Payyambalam and Ezhara,Kannur's vibrant Theyyam performances, a traditional ritualistic dance form, mesmerize spectators with their colorful costumes and spiritual fervor.

MOST POPULAR PLACES

Payyambalam Beach.

The Payyambalam Beach is an impeccably maintained destination in Kannur. This secluded location is known for providing one with the opportunity to simply relax or enjoy a family picnic in absolute tranquillity. Surfing and swimming options are also available for those who seek a little adventure. Being close to Kannur town has made it a favourite haunt for tourists and locals alike.

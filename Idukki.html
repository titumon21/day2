Idukki


Land of Dams

Idukki, a picturesque district in Kerala, India, boasts stunning landscapes and diverse attractions. Its crown jewel is the Idukki Arch Dam, an engineering marvel nestled amid lush hills, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding valleys. The district is a nature lover's paradise, with the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, where visitors can spot elephants, tigers, and various bird species amidst dense forests. Munnar, a hill station adorned with tea plantations, waterfalls, and trekking trails, lies within Idukki, captivating travelers with its serene beauty.
